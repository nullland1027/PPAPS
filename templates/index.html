Welcome to PPAPS

PPAPS is a protein pathogenicity prediction system for Bio-researchers. Protein, as an essential component of living organisms, affects the life activities of all species. various machine learning and deep learning algorithms are used to predict the pathogenicity of existing protein variation data. You can choose to predict animal or plant proteins by using several models like Random Forest, XGBoost, LightGBM and Attention Neural Network.
